### 2. Engine
Central story of the project — the unified crypto stack behind both platforms:
- **GopenPGP** — ProtonMail's crypto library, SEC Consult audit, MIT license
- **gomobile** — one Go codebase → xcframework (iOS) + AAR (Android) — visual diagram
- **RFC 9580** — AEAD (AES-256-OCB), Argon2 S2K, X25519
- **Zero telemetry** — no backend, no analytics, on-device only
- **670+ tests** — unified test matrix (Go + GnuPG interop)
